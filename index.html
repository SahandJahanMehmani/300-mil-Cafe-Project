About 300 Mil Cafe

Welcome to 300 Mil, where nostalgia meets comfort. Inspired by a love for old Persian music and vintage charm, our café is a haven of timeless beauty. Every corner is filled with relics from the past-aged furniture, antique décor, and a cozy atmosphere that feels like stepping back in time. On one side, a community board allows visitors to share their thoughts and feelings, leaving behind a piece of their story. Surrounded by plants and flowers, we invite you to enjoy our specialty drinks while the melodies of old Persian songs carry you away. At 300 ml, we're not just serving coffee; we're creating memories.
